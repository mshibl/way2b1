Cloud Operations Engineer

San Francisco, CA

The Role

Way2B1 is currently looking for a motivated, proactive and energetic individual to join the company as our first Cloud Operations Engineer. It is a critical role for us, and you will own and drive all cloud deployment that will directly contribute to our company’s sustained growth. This will be the first role exclusively focused on making deployment efficient, robust and secure at Way2B1- so if leadership and ownership is what you seek, look no further!

We have some interesting deployment challenges for you to roll up your sleeves to solve with a can-do attitude and out of the box solutions!

What you'll own

Deployment of all Way2B1 software to heterogeneous environments including AWS, Google Cloud Platform, Azure
Architect Way2B1's container strategy with optimization for developer throughput and testing
Influence network security from concept to ongoing improvements across server infrastructure
Close collaboration with the wider engineering team to both deliver platform improvements and provide subject-matter-expertise for other technical initiatives
Continuous improvements to our standard of engineering excellence by implementing best practices for coding, testing, deploying and communication

Our ideal candidate has

3+ years of experience in a developer operations role or similar function
AWS or comparable cloud computing experience
Container experience (docker and kubernetes are most ideal)
Build script expertise
Coding ability- we’re language agnostic, but would be particularly excited about Bash or Python
Ability to achieve security goals with a “Yes if/and/but” attitude
A hunger for collaboration (no lone wolves)
Integrity and honesty, ability to be trusted and to work with highly sensitive and confidential information

Bonus points for

A prior life as a software engineer and the ability to dust off those skills as needed
Being particularly excited about joining a smaller start-up and being excited to define their own impact on Way2B1
Excellent understanding of VPN's, firewalls, operating systems, security controls
Experience with managing penetration testing, bug bounties. and security event logging and analysis
Experience working with webpack, node.js or React

Oh yeah, and we can offer you

A tight-team knit of motivated, dedicated individuals who work together without ego
Competitive salary
Access to new technology for very cool and powerful clients
Opportunity to strengthen individual privacy and digital protections
A fully stocked kitchen in our Potrero Hill office
Access to, and engagement with leadership
