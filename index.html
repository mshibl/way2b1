Areas of Impact

Problems we solve

Families with complex lives face these common challenges:

  • - Family employees must work in a variety of roles and often outside of their core expertise
  • - Against the deluge of projects and incoming requests, it is difficult to establish proper procedures
  • - Information is siloed and poor history is maintained for key systems and decisions
  • - Time pressures, isolation, and lack of transparency block prioritization and feedback on key projects
  • - Outdated or inadequate technology impedes day to day workflows

Family office: A team that supports families, assisting in their investments, operations and personal well being. Family offices can be as small as an assistant or a childcare professional and as large as a small corporation that spans disparate domains.

Way2B1 was founded to address these problems and generate efficiencies, eliminate isolation, and enhance communication within the team as a whole, and between the family office and the family itself. And, perform all of these activities while preserving security and privacy.


With experience in building the most successful family offices in the US and technical expertise honed within Palantir - Way2B1 is solving these challenges utilizing the most current technology and the most effective operational methodology. As part of our quest we value: enjoyment in the journey, team investment, mentorship, and embracing the creed of the artisan, that is craftsmanship within our areas of expertise. We look for opportunities to help our teammates before ourselves. We are always thinking about how to help our families.
